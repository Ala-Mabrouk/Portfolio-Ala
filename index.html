First prize at the OpenGovDataHackathon2023

Royal Hotel in Hammamet, Tunisia

My team, comprised of Saber Ben Ali, Ghada Louhichi, and myself, achieved first place in the OpenGovDataHackathon2023, an event jointly organized by the World Bank, Ministry of Education in Tunisia, and the Electronic Governance Unit, among others. Throughout the intensive three-day competition, we faced formidable opposition from numerous talented teams. However, we were able to distinguish ourselves through our innovative approach and unwavering dedication. Our primary focus was on reshaping the future of education with our project entitled "New Generation-مدرسة دودي." Our victorious mobile application offers a range of features, including engaging short videos that deliver interactive and educational content for children. Additionally, it incorporates interactive storytelling and quizzes to assess their learning progress.

March 3 & 4 2023

First prize in STCCCV Cardiohackathon powered by Connect Innov

Laico Hotel Tunis, Tunisia

I had the privilege of taking part in the inaugural STCCCV Cardiohackathon, powered by Connect Innov, held on March 3rd and 4th, 2023, at the Laico Hotel in Tunis. Being recognized as the first-place winner was a tremendous honor for our team. Our challenge during the event was to design and develop "Sah-Tek".
SAH-TEK is a web/mobile application designed for monitoring chronic patients, offering features like a digital medical pillbox, therapeutic compliance tracking, remote progress monitoring, and digital medical records.
